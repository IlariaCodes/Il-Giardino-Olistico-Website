Il Giardino Olistico

The only Wellness Festival in Abruzzo

7-8 September

Avezzano

Join us for a weekend of relax!

The only Welness festival in Abruzzo

Following the success of last year's inaugural event, we are excited to announce the second edition of "Il Giardino Olistico"! Organized by Paola Munzi, the founder of TAMS, this festival aims to promote a holistic approach to wellness. We offer a diverse range of activities, including yoga, meditation, healthy eating, holistic practices, and alternative medicine. Through our program, attendees will have the opportunity to deepen their understanding of living in harmony with themselves — a concept that encompasses the body, mind , and soul. Join us for two inspiring days dedicated to wellness!

7-8 September
Avezzano, Castello Orsini

"Health is a state of complete harmony of the body, mind, and spirit." — B.K.S. Iyengar

Activity

Yoga

Yoga is a practice that seeks to create a harmonious union between the body, mind, and spirit, enhancing our consciousness not only of ourselves but also of the universe. Yoga is not only known for its physical benefits, such as increased flexibility, strength, improved posture, and better balance, but also for its mental and spiritual advantages. In fact, yoga helps reduce stress and enhances concentration, mindfulness, and self-awareness.

Put on something comfy, grab your mat, and come practice with us!

Meditation

Meditation is a mental practice that helps you slow down racing thoughts, release negativity, and bring a sense of calm to both mind and body, allowing you to experience inner peace. By using a combination of mental and physical techniques, meditation fosters mindfulness and self-awareness. During the festival, you will have the opportunity to deepen your understanding of this practice and enhance your ability to focus on the present moment.

Come and find your inner peace with us!

Holistic treatment

Holistic treatment is an integrative therapy that combines both traditional and alternative approaches, focusing on healing the individual as a whole. This broad term encompasses a wide range of techniques designed to promote overall well-being. At "Il Giardino Olistico", there will be six dedicated stands offering various holistic treatments, available throughout the day.
Each session will last 10-15 minutes, and no booking is required.

So, come and indulge in a little self-care!

Conference

We believe that living a mindful and conscious life requires knowledge. That's why, during these two days filled with practical activities, we've also included several conferences to explore topics that help cultivate a more balanced and healthy lifestyle. Our topics range from the importance of a healthy diet to discussions on autoimmune diseases and alternative therapies like acupuncture.
Each conference will last approximately one hour and will be held inside the Castle. No booking is required.

Come and join us on this journey of knowledge and growth!

"When mind, body, and spirit are in harmony, happiness is the natural result." — Deepak Chopra

Calendar

During the two-day festival, you’ll have the opportunity to attend both practical lessons and conferences, as they are scheduled at different times throughout the day. Please note that some of the practical lessons require advance booking. Holistic treatments, on the other hand, will be offered in six dedicated gazebos, where professionals will be available throughout the day to provide 10-15 minute mini-treatments with no booking.
Check our calendar for more details and to see which activities require booking.
